# Privacy

Clean Mac Desktop processes everything locally on your Mac.

The application:

- does not collect or transmit data;
- does not contain analytics, telemetry, advertising, accounts, activation, or DRM;
- does not access the network;
- does not read the names or contents of Desktop files;
- does not request macOS privacy permissions.

It reads and changes macOS's Desktop-item visibility preference and restarts Finder so macOS applies the change.
